Avalaki By Nandan Vasista


Avalaki or poha is a very popular Indian dish. It's very easy to prepare,yet very delicious too. Here are some ingriedients which you need in order to prepare it .

Ingriedients

  • avalaki
  • water
  • oil
  • kadale bele
  • udina bele
  • sasve
  • kay thure
  • kotumri
  • karibevu

Now we will prepare it in most easiest way and also see some variations of the dish

Procedure

  1. soak avalaki inside a chinadish using water for 5-10 minutes
  2. after 10 minutes, remove all the water and empty the countainer
  3. keep the same chinadish on medium flame . After small water droplets are converted into water vapours add some oil and let it heat up a bit
  4. then add some sasve,kadale bele and udina bele
  5. after these above ingriedients become golden brown in colour add some green chillies

      6. you can add some additional vegetables now , but my recomendation is not to add any

      any way here are some

    • onion
    • carrot
    • tomatoes
    • etc
  6. then add avalaki and mix it well
  7. after a few minutes add kotumri ,karibevu and some kaythuri
  8. mix it well again , you can add sugar a bit if you didn't add any vegetables like me
  9. Avalaki is ready to it.
